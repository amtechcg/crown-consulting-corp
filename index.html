Medicare & Medicaid

Government health programs are powerful—but notoriously complex. Our specialists guide individuals and organizations through Medicare Parts A, B, C, and D as well as Medicaid qualification, enrollment, and ongoing benefit optimization.

  • Medicare enrollment timing strategy
  • Part D prescription plan selection
  • Medicaid eligibility assessment

MediCap Supplement Plans

Medicare alone leaves significant gaps in coverage. Our MediCap (Medicare Supplement) policies are structured to cover deductibles, copayments, and coinsurance—protecting you from unexpected out-of-pocket costs when you need care most.

  • Gap analysis of existing Medicare coverage
  • Plan F, G, N comparison and selection
  • Carrier rate-shopping across all major issuers
  • Enrollment and ongoing plan management
